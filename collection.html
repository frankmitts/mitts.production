WELCOME TO "STAGE"

AN EVOLVING PROJECT TO UNEARTH THE MYSTERY OF WHO WE ARE IN OUR ART.

WHY STAGE

//Definition: A period or step in a process, activity, or development

As artists, we’re continually evolving our work. Not satisfied to remain static, we explore and experiment.
I believe we move through developmental cycles much like the stages of human development.

STAGE 01

Withdrawn from reality she immersed in a dream of flashing lights and designer fabrics. Forever reaching her goals.

STAGE 02

Sanctum. A place where one is free from intrusion. A sacred place to chanel your creative mind.

STAGE 03

In a society where expressing our true selves is becoming harder and harder, RAWNESS is needed. It’s needed in our emotions and who we are. To fully express ourselves we need to embracing are souls first. Embrace our light as much as our darkness.

LE PETIT DÉJEUNER

Morning. Irony of a beginning that starts with an end. A whole world in panic while routine breakfast and thoughts are here to see the day go by. Calm within chaos and purity within confinement.

FIGHT FOR THE LIGHT

"My biggest message to all of you is to be kind or simply be quiet. We know sticks and stones break our bones but words pierce and stay engraved in the mind and heart longer than most physical wounds. At the end of the day, we are ALL human, wanting nothing more than to be loved and accepted for exactly who we are. Hand in hand, heart to heart, we’re stronger when we aren’t ripping each other apart.”

– Victoria Lavigne, Founder of Fight For The Light
