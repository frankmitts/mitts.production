Branding

Rosie’s is a newly emerging restaurant that serves delicious southern food. What sets this place apart from the others is its unique pairing with a raw bar. Rosie’s has a great designed interior, recipe and atmosphere that will encourage everyone to come back. That being said, the restaurant uses red to demonstrate its energetic vibe paired with creating a more harmonious yet eye-catching design that would reflect the restaurant.

Setting the task

Okay, so we know this logo could use some improvement, and may not reflex what this restaurant is all about. Therefore, the task at hand. is creating an identity that will resonate with the costumes the better showcase the full experience of Rosie's

Drawing board

Now here we go! How to reflex their flamed grilled southern food with the pairing of a raw bar? After producing multiple logo ideas to the point of accumulating 16 Mc.Coffee stickers. I went to collect those free McDonald coffees. and after doodling out a couple extra sheets of sketches. I've finally landed on a concept.

Prototype

From the drawing board, I scanned and digitalised the logo. The reasoning behind this logo is incorporating the most used meat in southern food which is the pig and incorporate it with the flames to create their icon. The typographical part of the logo was created to bring those rustic, bold characteristics from the south.
