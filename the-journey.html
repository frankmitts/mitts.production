THE JOURNEY

ABOUT ME

Im a 20 year old creative, thats been around arts since I was a kid. Was in a specialez art program in highschool learning many different style every school day for 4years. On the side a played hockey and soccer.

MENTOR

In high school I was mentored after school. by Rick Lebrun, a old school graphic designer that did everthing buy hand. I've learned so much from airbrush to pencil drawing ads.

PHOTOGRAPHY

When traveling to England, I've discovered a passion for photography taken pictures of everything. Later on I was taken photos of equestrian competitions for a good 2 years. Theses days im a portrait and lifestyle photographer.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Was always a picky artist and liked having clean lines. Making sure things were positioned properly. This porgram called "focus" was a semester long focus of graphic design. I learned all the basic and got to work on a plutorial of diverse project and did a co-op at a print shop.

Photography

I photograph various types of shoots: portraits, event, lifestyle, and I hope to expand my portfolio in the near future working with more brands. I'm mostly a natural light photographer but will use flash if needed.
